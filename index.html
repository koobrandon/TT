How it works

Four things, one connected platform.

Every part of TECQTHINQ talks to every other part. Tap a card to go deeper.

01 · TEACH

Teaching support

AI helps educators plan lessons, build assessments, and run the classroom — cutting prep so teachers focus on students.

Think of it as a tireless teaching aide. It drafts lesson plans aligned to your objectives, suggests activities and worked examples, and prepares classroom-ready materials and rubrics. During delivery it can surface alternate explanations and pacing cues. The aim is simple: turn hours of prep into minutes, so a teacher's energy goes to the people in the room, not the paperwork.

Learn more +
02 · LEARN

Learning enhancement

Students get personalised, visual, interactive lessons and a 24/7 companion that explains things until they click.

Learning adapts to the learner instead of the other way round. Content is delivered visually and interactively, difficulty adjusts to each student, and the Buddy companion is always on hand to re-explain a tricky idea in a different way. The result is deeper understanding and better retention — because students stay active and curious rather than passively reading.

Learn more +
03 · MANAGE

Management systems

Administration, reporting, and workflow live in one dashboard, so institutions run smoothly without scattered tools.

Schools juggle enrolment, scheduling, attendance, grading, and reporting — usually across half a dozen disconnected systems. TECQTHINQ pulls them into one place, automates the repetitive parts, and keeps records consistent. Leaders see an accurate picture of the institution; staff stop re-keying the same data into different apps.

Learn more +
04 · PROTECT

Safe by design

Curated content, moderated communication, and child-safe spaces are built in from the start — not bolted on.

Safety isn't a setting you switch on later. Content is curated and filtered, communication runs through secure educational channels, interactions are moderated, and younger learners get guided, supervised exploration. The whole environment is designed so children can learn and create without exposure to the open, unmoderated web.

AI Lesson Generation

Automated content creation from a simple prompt.

Describe a topic, level, and goal — the platform drafts a complete, structured lesson: objectives, a clear explanation, worked examples, activities, and assessment questions. It matches tone and difficulty to the audience and stays aligned to curriculum, so educators edit and approve rather than build from scratch.

More +

Automated Assessments

Intelligent evaluation and instant feedback.

Create quizzes, tests, and rubrics in seconds. Objective questions mark themselves instantly and students get immediate, constructive feedback. Teachers receive aggregated results that pinpoint exactly where understanding broke down — not just a score.

More +

Knowledge Visualization

Complex ideas turned into clear visual structures.

Dense material becomes diagrams and structured visuals that show relationships at a glance. Seeing how ideas fit together helps learners build durable mental models instead of memorising isolated, disconnected facts.
